# Hello — and why I started writing

I've been making games for a few years now, and I keep running into the same realization: the things I learn the hard way never get written down anywhere. So I'm starting this blog to fix that.

## What you'll find here

- **Devlogs** — what I'm building, the design decisions, the parts that broke
- **Unity tips** — small things that took me too long to figure out
- **Game jam writeups** — post-mortems from the jams I join
- **Tooling** — editor scripts, asset pipelines, and the dumb little tools that save hours

## What I'm working on right now

I'm building a game called **Finn** for Steam — a cozy adventure about a goofy axolotl. It's the biggest scope I've taken on solo, and I'm trying to document the process in real time instead of writing it all up after the fact.

If you're into Unity, gamedev, or just curious about the chaos behind shipping a game, stick around. New posts every couple of weeks.
